## Project Summary:
This project analyzes Indian Railway accident data from 1990 to 2024 to identify patterns, trends, and safety concerns.
Key insights:
- Uttar Pradesh and Bihar show the highest accident frequency.
- Most accidents are due to derailments and signal failures.
- Sundays and monsoon months are risk-prone periods.
